Guide

Selecting wells

  • Click a well to select it. Ctrl+click to toggle individual wells. Shift+click to select a rectangular range.
  • Click & drag on empty space to rubber-band select a region.
  • Click & drag directly on wells to paint-fill them with the active type.
  • Click row letters or column numbers to select entire rows/columns.
  • The canvas well-selector grid (in the selection panel) lets you click or drag to select wells interactively.

Assigning well types

  • Select wells, then click a well type in the Assignment panel that appears at the bottom.
  • Or activate a type in the top palette and paint directly onto the plate.
  • Add custom types with the + Custom button — choose any name and colour.

Labels and dilutions

  • With wells selected, type a label in the Label field to annotate them.
  • For a row or column selection, the Dilution series tool applies concentration labels automatically (choose start concentration, fold factor, and direction).

Export

  • Click Export PNG to download a 600 DPI image. Text auto-fits inside wells and wraps to two lines if needed.
  • The export always uses a white background regardless of the current dark/light theme.
