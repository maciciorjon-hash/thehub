LabMate
Calculators across Mol Biology, Cell Biology, CRISPR, Proteomics, Biophysics & Struct Bio. Dilutions, buffers, MW, Tm, cell culture & more.
Dilutions · Buffers · MW · PROTAC →
Degradation Explorer
Paste Excel data to explore DC50 vs Dmax by target & series. Filter compounds, hover for details, browse the full table.
Scatter · Table · DC50 · Dmax →
Lab Designer
Design 96/384-well plates with dilution series, compound labels & colour-coded types. Gel lane designer with marker reference.
96/384-well · Dilutions · Gel · PNG →
Helix
Translate DNA/RNA, find ORFs, reverse complement, calculate Tm, align sequences with mismatch highlighting & browse the lab vector library.
Translate · Tm · ORF · Vectors →
Protein Tools
Paste a protein sequence to get MW, extinction coefficient (ε280), pI, GRAVY score, instability index & amino acid composition.
MW · ε280 · pI · GRAVY →
Spectra
Quantify protein by A280, check A260/A280 purity, fit Bradford & BCA standard curves & process plate reader absorbance data.
A280 · Bradford · BCA · Plate reader →
LDI
Score degrader ligase dependency from CRBN-KO & VHL-KO curves. AUC-based LDI from 4PL parameters — +1 = CRBN, −1 = VHL.
CRBN · VHL · AUC · LDI score →
Iceberg
Cryostorage inventory for −80°C & liquid N₂. Rack/box hierarchy, 96-well box grids with per-vial hover info, Excel-style table view, cell-line colouring, bulk paste, XLSX export.
Cell lines · Passage · Freeze media · Inventory →
Cuppa

Fabricata™

p-values · Significance · Results →
Beacon

BRET · Plate Map · Dose-Response →
Lumina
