v1.0.28 — 12 Jun 2026
● Echo 4PL fitter upgrade (all 4 of Ryan-via-Gemini's suggestions, evaluated and shipped):
  ◦ Multi-start now uses 5 evenly-spaced seeds + a 6th "X at YMID" seed targeting the empirical inflection
  ◦ λ damping now Fletcher-adaptive: λ ×= max(1/3, 1−(2ρ−1)3) on accept (was fixed ÷3); reject grows λ ×= 2
  ◦ Initial Top / Bottom = median of top-3 / bottom-3 Y values (robust to single noisy extremes; was raw Ymax/Ymin)
  ◦ Convergence now uses gradient norm ||JTr||∞ < 1e-8·(1+|SSR|) as the primary criterion (catches flat-valley cases where |Δp| stalled early). |Δp| backup at 1e-10. Max 200 iters unchanged
